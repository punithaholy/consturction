Architectural Project Description:

This is Ortho based speciality hospital with other consultations.

In Second Floor- operation theatres and ICU

In Third and Fourth Floor – Residential for the Dean

Area: Approx. 24000ft² of total space

Structural Project Description:

This project is designed by Usha Builders & Engineers by using Staad Pro. Bentley V8i.

The soil report submitted by M/s. Atlanta.

The building is designed by Eng. TiroumalaiSinouvassin who is having PPA registered Structural Engineer.

The brief description of structural details as follows-

-The building is designed based on the IS 456, SP 16, etc., and designed for B+G+4. And the pile and pile cap adopted for foundation as mentioned by soil report. The detailed drawing prepared based on the IS specification and executed by us. For the Basement, outer wall is constructed as RCC wall and located in below 4’ from road level. The proper water proofing is done by the sub-contractor and followed by us.

The M20 concrete is used for all items except pile and pile cap which is used as M30. The manual mix is done by using Mixer machine with lifting equipment’s. The waterproof chemical used for roof slab to avoid leakage. The centring and shuttering work is done by using span and jack system. The steel used as Fe 500.

Construction methods:

The method of construction is used as framed structure method where the foundation is Pile and Pile cap, basement wall is RCC wall, etc., All the mixes such as mortar and all concrete done by mixer machine. Sometimes the manual mix is also done by using the measurement box system.

The residential floor is made as chettinad style with pillars and Athankudi tiles.

Pest Control is done by PCI pest Control Pvt. Ltd. For all the areas.

The water proofing is done by United Constructive Pvt. Ltd Company from Chennai. The membrane system is used for basement and liquid system is used for all toilets with proper chamfer. And all sunken portion liquid system and landscaping area is done by liquid system and protected with geo textile sheet. And screed concrete is done over the geo textile work.

All walls are done by using AAC blocks with necessary meshes used. The wall plastering done by mixing water proof chemical in the mortar.

The floors for corridor and Reception area is done by Marble and all the rooms by Marbonite tiles. The grouting is done by grout material with proper spacing.

All windows made by UPVC with double glass to reduce the wood for green concept and fixed grill with powder coating paint. All doors are made by Teak wood with flush door and imported fittings.

All Electrical works done by M/s. EMMPOWER OCNTROLS Pvt Ltd. The separate electrical panel room provided for panel boards for easy convenient. The separate transform located for this project and two sets of 125KVA genset placed for non breaking of powersupply. In addition, the UPS inverter system also provided.

All Plumbing pipe lines by CPVC and all fittings by Jaquar. The water supplied to the hospital is purified by softener and for drinking the separate RO system provided. The common water heater provided to avoid waste and save power in the roof and connected to all floor toilets.

The proper sump and OHT is provided to full fill the fire fighting requirement and the septic tank provided based on the calculation. All wall painting works by Asian interior emulsion based on two coats of wall putty and primer.

The HAVC work done for all the rooms and reception hall, canteen, Xray room, Physiotherapy, etc

All the areas are monitored by CCTV which is provided in all areas.

Practice adopted:

Soil investigation is done report submitted by M/s. Atlanta and result given as pile foundation at the depth of 22m from the NGL. And the pile is placed at 22m from the NGL.

The site is investigated and soil report has been studied before starting the design.
