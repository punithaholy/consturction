ABOUT US

USHA Builders & Engineers, the developing construction company in the Puducherry Construction Industry, was established in 2015. Previously, it was established in 2009 as partnership firm as THIRU Constructions. USHA Builders & Engineers has undertaken a program of construction, including but not limited to: Residential, Commercial, Resorts, Hotel and Hotel Interiors, Residential apartments and Communities, Luxury Villas, Industrial Projects, Hospitals, etc.

In the past few years, USHA Builders has diversified geographically and in addition to the Puducherry, Usha Builders started working in other cities such as: Chennai, Virudachalam, Villupuram, Auroville, etc.

Eng. TIROUMALAI SINOUVASSIN, CEO of the company, completed the degree in 2001 Pondicherry Engg. College and holding the Registered Structural Engineer ( Grade-1) and License from PWD Class-III. And started his career from Puducherry, VP construction, Sri Velaayutham Constructions, etc and travelled to UAE, Dubai worked as Site engineer in Alfa Constructions for three years and Project Engineer in Arabtec Constructions Pvt. Ltd., which is the topmost company in the Middle east and completed the Project “ DIFC truck tunnel”, “Anantara and Tiara Hotel Project, Palm Jumeriah – the beach resort hotel project in inside the sea”, etc. and Mrs.USHARANI TIROUMALAI, Chairman of the company, completed the degree in 2004 and maintaining the administration of the company and projects.
